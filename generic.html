About Us

NexGen Software is a student lead organization that strives to develop vital tech skills in students wishing to participate in competitions such as hackathons, science fairs, etc. NexGen specializes in providing mentorship to students who have existing project ideas but have trouble implementing them. We provide personalized assistance through the various stages of competition including design, creation, and presentation. Our mentorship is broken down into 4 different tracks). These tracks are formed with the purpose of providing students with the full experience needed to excel in these stem competitions.


Our goals are to:

assist our members in implementing technologies learned in various other personal projects

train our members for a variety of new skills within tech

host hackathons and showcases, allowing our members to reap the benefits of their newfound skills and receive recognition for their projects

supply students with bootcamp style classes and developing vital stem skills in the process

provide the comprehensive preparation to excel in these competitions

connect students with competitions tailored to their interests and skillsets
